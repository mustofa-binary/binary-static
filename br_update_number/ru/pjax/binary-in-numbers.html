Binary.com в цифрах

Binary.com - ведущая мировая компания по торговле бинарными опционами, работающая с 2000 года.

Активные клиенты

Our active client base is expected to reach over 790,000 total clients this year.

Количество контрактов

We're projected to register over 1.1 billion transactions this year, bringing the total number of contracts that were bought and sold on our platform since inception to over 2.8 billion.

Число сотрудников и внештатных работников

We've grown in size over the years - both in terms of manpower and offices. We currently have over 380 employees across 6 offices in Malaysia, Malta, Paraguay, Dubai, Rwanda and Cyprus. Further growth in headcount is expected this year to cater to the increased demand in our products and services.
